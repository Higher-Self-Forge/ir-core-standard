# Consent Materials Checklist (FPIC Support)

<!--
INSTRUCTIONS:
- Use this checklist to ensure consent sessions are informed and documented.
- Attach or reference materials; avoid forcing digital-only workflows.
-->

## 1. Purpose and scope clarity
- [ ] Purpose statement is clear and non-technical
- [ ] Data categories are listed with examples
- [ ] Processing activities are listed (collect/store/train/infer/share)
- [ ] Beneficiaries and access boundaries are clear
- [ ] Retention and deletion/offboarding plan is explained

## 2. Risks and safeguards
- [ ] Known risks are explained (misuse, re-identification, bias, security)
- [ ] Safeguards are explained (boundaries, audit log, access controls)
- [ ] Limitations are explained (accuracy limits, uncertainty, failure modes)
- [ ] Complaint / incident channel is provided

## 3. Rights and choices
- [ ] Revocation mechanism is explained
- [ ] Conditions and restrictions are supported
- [ ] Consent triggers are explained (what changes require re-consent)
- [ ] Alternatives are discussed (including declining participation)

## 4. Accessibility and language
- [ ] Materials exist in relevant languages (e.g., es-PE, quz)
- [ ] Interpretation support is available if needed
- [ ] Visual aids are accessible (low literacy options)

## 5. Evidence and recordkeeping
- [ ] Meeting minutes recorded (or equivalent community-legible record)
- [ ] Signatures/thumbprints captured (as appropriate)
- [ ] Evidence references include hashes where possible
- [ ] Machine-readable consent record created (optional but recommended)
